# 🌍 Travoro  
### ✨ AI Powered Travel Planning, Safety & Group Coordination App  

---

## 📌 Overview  

**Travoro** is a modern Android application built to simplify travel planning by bringing everything into one place.  
It enables users to plan trips, collaborate with friends, manage expenses, and ensure safety through real-time location sharing.  

Unlike traditional travel apps, **Travoro focuses on group coordination, safety, and smart planning in a single platform.**

---

## 🚀 Features  

### 🧭 Trip Creation & Management  
Create and manage trips with destination, dates, budget, and description.  

### 👥 Group Collaboration  
Invite friends and plan trips together in real-time.  

### 📍 Live Location Sharing (Broadcast Telemetry)  
Share real-time location with trip members for better coordination and safety.  

### 💰 Expense Tracking & Splitting  
Record expenses and split costs transparently among group members.  

### 🗓️ Itinerary Planning  
Organize day-wise travel plans for a smooth experience.  

### 🌦️ Weather Forecast  
Get weather updates for your destination.  

### 🤖 Smart Recommendations  
Discover hotels, restaurants, and places based on your trip.  

### 🔔 Push Notifications  
Receive real-time updates using Firebase Cloud Messaging (FCM).
